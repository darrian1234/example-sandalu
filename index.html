About Sandalau Resorts

Your Sanctuary in the Wild

Nestled on the borders of Yala National Park, Sandalau Resorts is a boutique haven that offers an intimate connection with Sri Lanka's most celebrated wildlife sanctuary. Our eco-luxury bungalows blend traditional Sri Lankan architecture with contemporary comforts.

Each residence is thoughtfully designed to provide privacy and tranquility, with panoramic views of the surrounding wilderness. Awaken to the symphony of the jungle and spend your days exploring Asia's premier wildlife destination.
